About

Hi! My name is Emily, and I am an artist and software engineer located in San Francisco, CA.

I grew up in Minneapolis, Minnesota, home to green, humid, lake-hopping summers and snowy, quiet winters. I've called San Francisco home for the past five years and absolutly love living so close to the Pacific Ocean with her tide pools, rocky coasts, and seaweedy beaches.

In 2017, I graduated from Carleton College with Bachelor's Degrees in Computer Science and Studio Art. Nowadays, I code by day and print by night. When I'm not hunched over my laptop or a new linolium block, you can find me running in GGP, unwrapping a new chocolate bar, or wandering through one of my favorite independent bookstores.

Although my most recent works have focused on and around printmaking, I'm excited to get back to ceramics, drawing, painting, and kinetic sculpture soon. Keep in touch to see what I'll be up to next!
